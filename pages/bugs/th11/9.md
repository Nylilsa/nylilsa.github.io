# Extra Leaderboard Crash
[hr_major]

## [specs]
[hr_minor]  

* **Versions**: [yes]1.00a[/yes] 
* **Difficulty**: [no]Easy[/no] - [no]Normal[/no] - [no]Hard[/no] - [no]Lunatic[/no] - [yes]Extra[/yes]
* **Mode**: [yes]Main game[/yes] - [no]Practice mode[/no]
* **Shottype**: [yes]ReimuA[/yes] - [yes]ReimuB[/yes] - [yes]ReimuC[/yes] - [yes]MarisaA[/yes] - [yes]MarisaB[/yes] - [yes]MarisaC[/yes]

## [what]
[hr_minor]

If you clear the Extra Stage with a lower score than your leaderboard, your leaderboard being full, the gameover options are mistakenly shown. This includes the ``Continue`` option; selecting ``Continue`` crashes the game

## [how]
[hr_minor]

Selecting ``Continue`` after clearing Extra crashes the game.
