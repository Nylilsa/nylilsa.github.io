# Koishi Card Desync
[hr_major]

## [specs]
[hr_minor]

* **Versions**: [yes]1.00a[/yes]
* **Difficulty**: [yes]Easy[/yes] - [yes]Normal[/yes] - [yes]Hard[/yes] - [yes]Lunatic[/yes] - [no]Extra[/no]
* **Mode**: [yes]Main game[/yes] - [unknown]Practice mode[/unknown] - [no]Spell practice[/no]
* **Shottype**: [yes]Reimu[/yes] - [yes]Marisa[/yes] - [yes]Sakuya[/yes] - [yes]Sanae[/yes]
* **Cards**:
[img=assets/th18-cards/27.png]

## [what] 
[hr_minor]

Having bought the Koishi card may desync the game.

## [how]
[hr_minor]

1. Purchase the Koishi card,
2. Restart the stage through Esc + R
3. Ram into an enemy, you will not die.

Steps 1 and 2 are very common with shop manipulation (insert link to page), while step 3 is something that is often done accidentally. 

## [why]
[hr_minor]

The effect of the Koishi card lingers between stage resets. This means that if you have used shop manipulation and run into an enemy, you will not die. In the replay however the game considers you to not be using the Koishi card, thus killing you.
