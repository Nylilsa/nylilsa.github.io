# Momoyo Card Desync
[hr_major]

## [specs]
[hr_minor]

* **Versions**: [yes]1.00a[/yes]
* **Difficulty**: [yes]Easy[/yes] - [yes]Normal[/yes] - [yes]Hard[/yes] - [yes]Lunatic[/yes] - [no]Extra[/no]
* **Mode**: [yes]Main game[/yes] - [no]Practice mode[/no] - [no]Spell practice[/no]
* **Shottype**: [yes]Reimu[/yes] - [yes]Marisa[/yes] - [yes]Sakuya[/yes] - [yes]Sanae[/yes]
* **Cards**:
[img=assets/th18-cards/54.png]

## [what] 
[hr_minor]

The game is likely to desync if you have the Nue or the Momoyo card equipped.

## [how]
[hr_minor]

Equip the Momoyo card during a non-Extra credit.

## [why]
[hr_minor]

When a new stage is loaded, the card's counter automatically resets to 0. This likely desyncs the replay.
