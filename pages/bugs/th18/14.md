# Equipment Cards Desync
[hr_major]

## [specs]
[hr_minor]

* **Versions**: [yes]1.00a[/yes]
* **Difficulty**: [yes]Easy[/yes] - [yes]Normal[/yes] - [yes]Hard[/yes] - [yes]Lunatic[/yes] - [no]Extra[/no]
* **Mode**: [yes]Main game[/yes] - [no]Practice mode[/no] - [no]Spell practice[/no]
* **Shottype**: [yes]Reimu[/yes] - [yes]Marisa[/yes] - [yes]Sakuya[/yes] - [yes]Sanae[/yes]
* **Cards**:
[img=assets/th18-cards/20.png] [img=assets/th18-cards/51.png]

## [what] 
[hr_minor]

The game is likely to desync if you have the Nue or the Misumaru card equipped.

## [how]
[hr_minor]

Equip either the Misumaru or Nue card.

## [why]
[hr_minor]

Both Nue's and Misumaru's card create options that oscilate. The time of the oscilation is not saved in the replay. This means when you load the replay from non-stage 1, the game desyncs.
