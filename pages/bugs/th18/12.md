## [what] 
[hr_minor]

The playtime in the player data may be incorrect.

## [how]
[hr_minor]

This can be achieved by playing credits on Easy, Normal, Hard or Lunatic Mode.

## [why]
[hr_minor]

The playtime adds the time from the previous stage you played on. For example, if you cleared Lunatic, and Stage 6 took you 4 minutes to clear, then 4 minutes will be added to your current playtime: the playtime of stages 1 through 5 will not be counted.
